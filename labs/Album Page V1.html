Let It Be

The Beatles

Release Date: May 8, 1970

Let It Be is the twelfth and final studio album by The Beatles. It was released almost a month after the group's break-up. Like many of their other releases, it was a number one album in many countries.

Band Members

  1. John Lennon - Lead and Backing Vocals, Guitar
  2. Paul McCartney - Lead and Backing Vocals, Bass Guitar, Keyboards
  3. George Harrison - Lead and Backing Voals, Lead and Rhythm Guitars
  4. Ringo Starr - Drums and Percussion
  5. George Martin - Producer
